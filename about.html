About Botanical Colour

Botanical Colour is a community-rooted project exploring the magic of natural dyeing through plants, art, and shared knowledge.

This archive and interactive swatch tool was created by Makepeace Studio and collaborators to honour the living traditions of plant-based colour, seasonal rhythms, and slow making.

Each swatch represents a plant dye — click them to discover artwork, recipes, and workshop memories.

Have something to share? Get in touch or join a workshop.
